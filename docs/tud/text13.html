Well-lit interiors

Design Reference Light Shelves

British High Commission Colombo

Since the NAUPRSOSTIR Air Tube is very dark from inside, light shelves can be used to bring in natural light. In the British commission, sunlight enters the building through the light shelves which gets reflected by reflectors below it and then diffused inside the building.
